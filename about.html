About Ian Koina

I’m a cybersecurity expert, AI privacy architect, and digital strategist based in Nairobi, Kenya. Through ByteForge, I blend tech, policy, and design to build digital tools that work for people and protect their data.

From leading privacy frameworks for African states to launching forensic tools like Deep Trace, my mission is simple: build secure, smart, and scalable tech for the next-gen digital ecosystem.

I also consult for startups, institutions, and government entities on how to navigate the complex digital compliance space—from Kenya’s Data Protection Act to EU’s GDPR, and everything in between.
